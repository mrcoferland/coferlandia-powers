# Opportunity Report

## 1. Executive Summary
Agentic monitoring dashboard for local LLM/Ollama usage. Educational example only; no income is guaranteed.

## 2. Opportunity
A lightweight dashboard that watches local LLM usage, failures, latency, disk pressure, and model performance for developers running Ollama or similar local stacks.

## 3. Why Now
Local AI usage is increasing and developers need simple operational visibility without enterprise observability cost.

## 4. Evidence
Facts to verify: public docs, changelogs, app reviews, community complaints, and comparable products. This example intentionally avoids fake source claims.

## 5. Source Quality
Use `source-quality-scoring` before acting. Prefer primary documentation and fresh community evidence over promotional blog posts.

## 6. Target Customer
Independent developers, small AI labs, and teams running local inference experiments.

## 7. Business Model
Open-source core plus paid hosted alerts, support, or team dashboard.

## 8. Technical MVP
Python collector, SQLite database, simple web dashboard, cron-based checks, and optional Discord/Telegram alerts.

## 9. Unit Economics
Use ranges. Expected early costs: hosting, data/API calls, domain, and Diego's time. Validate willingness to pay before scaling.

## 10. Risks
Crowded devtools market, weak willingness to pay, local environment variability, and privacy expectations.

## 11. 48-Hour Experiment
Create a one-page demo, collect 5 target-user conversations, and test one core automated output.

## 12. Kill Criteria
Kill if target users do not recognize the pain, data is unavailable, legal/platform risk is unacceptable, or no one commits to a concrete next step.

## 13. Final Score

Scores:
- Revenue potential: 1-5
- Speed to validate: 1-5
- Technical fit with Diego: 1-5
- Automation potential: 1-5
- Data availability: 1-5
- Capital required: 1-5, inverted
- Risk level: 1-5, inverted
- Distribution difficulty: 1-5, inverted

Final score is heuristic, not objective truth.

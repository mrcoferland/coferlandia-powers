# Opportunity Reporting

## Purpose
Create the final structured opportunity report from specialist analyses.

## When to use this skill
Use after scouting, source scoring, market analysis, CFO modeling, MVP design, and skeptic review have produced enough evidence for a decision.

## Inputs expected
- Opportunity summary.
- Evidence and source quality scores.
- Market, CFO, Builder, and Skeptic outputs.
- Standard scoring model values.

## Procedure
1. Separate facts, assumptions, hypotheses, and recommendations.
2. Use the mandatory report structure.
3. Apply the standard scoring model and label it heuristic.
4. Include a 48-hour experiment and kill criteria.
5. Choose exactly one final recommendation: BUILD, TEST, WAIT, or KILL.

## Mandatory report format
```markdown
# Opportunity Report

## 1. Executive Summary

## 2. Opportunity

## 3. Why Now

## 4. Evidence

## 5. Source Quality

## 6. Target Customer

## 7. Business Model

## 8. Technical MVP

## 9. Unit Economics

## 10. Risks

## 11. 48-Hour Experiment

## 12. Kill Criteria

## 13. Final Score

Scores:

- Revenue potential:
- Speed to validate:
- Technical fit with Diego:
- Automation potential:
- Data availability:
- Capital required:
- Risk level:
- Distribution difficulty:

## 14. Recommendation

BUILD / TEST / WAIT / KILL
```

## Safety constraints
Do not overhype. State uncertainty. Include legal, financial, privacy, security, and platform risks when relevant.

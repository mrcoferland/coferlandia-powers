# Crypto / DeFi Research

## Purpose
Research crypto and DeFi as sources of product opportunities, data products, dashboards, alerts, compliance tooling, and research automation. Do not speculate or execute trades.

## When to use this skill
Use when an opportunity involves protocols, TVL, liquidity, fees, tokenomics, token unlocks, bridges, stablecoins, hacks, custody, regulatory issues, on-chain dashboards, or DeFi risk alerts.

## Inputs expected
- Protocol, theme, chain, or narrative.
- Product hypothesis, if any.
- Data sources to inspect.

## Procedure
1. Identify protocol/theme and opportunity type.
2. Collect public data points: TVL, volume, liquidity, fees, revenue, incidents, unlocks, bridge/stablecoin exposure, and regulatory context.
3. Separate narrative from measured evidence.
4. Prefer non-financial product opportunities: intelligence dashboards, alerts, research automation, compliance tools, and data products.
5. Review smart contract, custody, market, regulatory, narrative, and data-quality risks.

## Output format
```text
Protocol / theme:
Opportunity type:
Data points:
Narrative:
Economic model:
Technical angle:
Risks:
Non-financial product opportunity:
Recommendation:
```

## Safety constraints
Never execute trades, sign transactions, move funds, initiate swaps, provide personalized investment advice, promote pump-and-dump, encourage market manipulation, or handle private keys or seed phrases.

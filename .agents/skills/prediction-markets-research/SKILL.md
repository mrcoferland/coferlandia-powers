# Prediction Markets Research

## Purpose
Use prediction markets as intelligence sources, not as betting instructions.

## When to use this skill
Use when events, public expectations, forecasts, or market-implied probabilities may reveal content, dashboard, alerting, research, or data-product opportunities.

## Inputs expected
- Market/event/theme.
- Relevant market links or candidate questions.
- Product or content hypothesis, if any.

## Procedure
1. Capture market/event and implied probability.
2. Identify the narrative and why it matters.
3. Compare public expectation with available evidence.
4. Look for data gaps, dashboard opportunities, alerting opportunities, content opportunities, and information-arbitrage opportunities.
5. Review liquidity, manipulation, bias, and platform risk.

## Output format
```text
Market / event:
Implied probability:
Signal:
Why it matters:
Opportunity:
Possible product:
Risks:
Recommendation:
```

## Safety constraints
Do not execute bets, recommend betting, move funds, or present market prices as objective truth.

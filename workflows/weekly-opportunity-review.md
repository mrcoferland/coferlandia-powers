# Weekly Opportunity Review Workflow

Purpose: rank the week’s opportunities and select one for deeper validation.

1. Collect all opportunity cards and reports from the week.
2. Consolidate duplicates and merge overlapping evidence.
3. Remove ideas already marked KILL unless new evidence changes the decision.
4. Ask `cfo-unit-economics` for economics on shortlisted ideas.
5. Ask `skeptic-risk` for adversarial review.
6. Score opportunities with the standard scoring model.
7. Rank by final score, confidence, and Diego-fit.
8. Select one opportunity for deep dive or MVP validation.
9. Archive WATCH items and kill weak ideas with written reasons.

Output: ranked list, one selected deep dive, killed ideas, open questions, and next action.

# Daily Scout Workflow

Purpose: discover fresh public signals for fully online, agentic, automatable opportunities.

1. Define today's search scope and excluded unsafe categories.
2. Search for 10 new signals across GitHub, HN, Reddit, Product Hunt, Indie Hackers, marketplaces, app stores, API announcements, SaaS pricing changes, crypto/DeFi data, and compliance changes.
3. Group signals by vertical.
4. Use `source-quality-scoring` for evidence that could influence a decision.
5. Filter to 3 candidate opportunities using: evidence quality, Diego-fit, automation potential, cheap validation, and legal/ethical acceptability.
6. Create a short `templates/opportunity-card.md` entry for each candidate.
7. Mark each candidate as WATCH, DEEP-DIVE, or IGNORE.
8. Escalate DEEP-DIVE candidates to `workflows/deep-dive.md`.

Safety: reject signals involving illegal hacking, fraud, phishing, credential abuse, market manipulation, or autonomous financial execution.

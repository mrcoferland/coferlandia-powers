# Kill or Build Workflow

Purpose: make a final decision after research or validation.

Review:
- Evidence for.
- Evidence against.
- Costs.
- Risks.
- Fit with Diego.
- Validation speed.
- Opportunity cost.
- Legal, privacy, security, platform, and financial concerns.

Decision options:
- BUILD: evidence is strong and MVP scope is clear.
- TEST: promising but needs a cheap validation step.
- WAIT: timing or evidence is incomplete; define what to watch.
- KILL: weak, unsafe, too costly, too risky, or poor fit.

Every decision must include reasoning, next action, and kill criteria.

# Code of Conduct

We want this project to be practical, respectful, and safe.

Contributors should:

- Be kind and professional.
- Welcome different levels of experience.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Keep discussions focused on ethical opportunity research and useful software.

Unacceptable behavior may result in moderation or removal from project spaces.
